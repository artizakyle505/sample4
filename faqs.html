FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Here are three simple but effective maintenance tips to help you preserve the beauty and durability of your furnitures:

What types of furniture do you sell?
We offer a wide range of furniture including sofas, beds, dining sets, wardrobes, office tables, shelves, and more. We also carry both modern and classic styles to fit any home.
Do you offer delivery services?
Yes, we offer delivery services within Metro Manila and nearby provinces. Delivery fees vary depending on your location and the size of the item.
Do you offer warranty?
Yes, all our furniture items come with a 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. This does not cover damages caused by misuse, accidents, or wear and tear.
Can I return or exchange an item?
Yes, returns or exchanges are allowed within 7 days upon delivery, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging. Custom-made items are non-returnable.
Do you accept installment payments?
Yes, we offer installment plans through select credit cards and partner financing companies. Just ask our staff or check out our online payment options for details.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery usually takes 3–7 business days after your order is confirmed. For custom or made-to-order items, delivery may take 2–3 weeks.
